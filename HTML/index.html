What is 1030 Shisa nyama all about...

1030 Shisa nyama, originating from the Dennilton postal code is the up and coming shisa nyama located in the township of Elandsdoring C (RDP). The main goal was to bring the best taste ekasi at affordable prices. The aim is to enjoy your meal, not to sweat your pockets.

The Shisa nyama will operate under lockdown protocols as set by the government.
